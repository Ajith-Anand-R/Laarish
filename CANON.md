## 3. THE 5 RULES (every gardener follows — global, all plants)

1. **5 MINUTES EVERY DAY** — Every single day, just 5 minutes. Mist, check, cheer them on. Never skip a day during Phase 1. ❤️
2. **HOW TO WATER** — Phase 1: Mist with **Misty only**, 10–15 sprays from 10 cm above, never pour directly. Phase 2 (grow bag): pour with **Cuppy** slowly in circles around inside edge of the bag — never in the centre. Wait for each round to soak. Watch for drainage from bottom holes — that means it's working.
3. **SUNLIGHT** — All four plants need 4–6 hours of direct sun every day. Warm sunny window or outdoor spot. No cold spots — **cold = no sprouting**.
4. **THINNING** — If two seeds sprout in the same cup, snip the weaker one at soil level with scissors. **Never pull** — pulling disturbs the roots of the one you're keeping.
5. **THE GRADUATION RULE** — Never move a plant to the grow bag early. All 4 Graduation Signs must be present before the move. Missing even one? Wait 3–4 more days.

**The 4 Graduation Signs (all plants):** ① True leaves (2 sets) ② Strong/thick upright stem (Chilly: naturally slim — upright matters more than thickness) ③ White roots through cup walls ④ Height 80–100 mm (check with Diggy; Chilly may need an extra week — be patient!).

## 4. Soil Guide (Phase reference)

**Prepare Corky (all plants):**
1. Soak Corky in plain water 5 minutes — let it go soft and dark.
2. Add 5 ml coco peat, spread flat at bottom of wet cup.
3. Mix in a small bowl: **90 ml black soil + 15 ml red soil + 45 ml vermicompost**.
4. Pour mixed soil onto coco peat, press gently, leave **10 mm space** at top.
5. Should feel like dark chocolate cake crumbs — not dry, not muddy.

**Grow bag mix (Phase 2, scoops):**
| Ingredient | Tommy | Okki | Chilly | Methi |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Garden soil | 7 | 7 | 5 | 3 |
| Vermicompost | 4 | 4 | 3 | 2 |
| Dry coco peat | 1 + water | 1 + water | 1 + water | ½ + water |
| Water after planting | 400 ml (4 Cuppys) | 400 ml (4 Cuppys) | 400 ml (4 Cuppys) | 200 ml (2 Cuppys) |

**Before filling grow bag:** ① Punch 4–6 holes at bottom corners FIRST ② Add water to dry coco peat, wait 5 minutes — watch it expand! ③ Combine all in a big bucket, mix with Diggy 2 minutes until dark & crumbly ④ Fill bag, leave 50 mm space at top.

## 5. Per-plant canon

### 🍅 TOMMY — THE CLIMBER (Tomato)
- At a glance: nursery mix Corky + soil · seed depth **6 mm** (Diggy's ruler) · sprouts **5–7 days** · grow bag 12"×12" LDPE · harvest: many tomatoes!
- **Super power:** bury **2/3 of the stem** underground → More Roots, More Water, More Food, More Tomatoes. "It looks extreme. It is exactly right."
- Phase 1 Nursery: ① Soak Corky 5 mins ② Fill with nursery mix, leave 10 mm space ③ Poke ONE hole 6 mm deep (check with Diggy) ④ Drop in 2 tomato seeds, cover gently ⑤ Label cup (TOMMY + date) ⑥ Mist with Misty.
- Milestones: Day 5–7 sprouts appear! · Week 3–4 Thinning Day (snip weaker sprout; one cup = one plant) · Week 5–7 true leaves ("Tommy is getting strong!") · Weeks 10–12 flowers, green tomatoes, red and ripe.
- Phase 2 Big Move: ① 12"×12" LDPE grow bag ② punch 4–6 holes ③ **snip Tommy's lowest leaves off first — buried leaves rot** ④ bury 2/3 of stem deep, place cup in bag ⑤ fill soil to 10 mm below rim, top leaves stick out ⑥ water 400 ml (4 Cuppys) slowly in circles.
- Phase 3: Weeks 5–7 yellow flowers · 7–9 green tomatoes form · 10–12+ turn red and ripe · HARVEST OFTEN — "Pick when fully red. Sweet, juicy, perfect!"
- Badges: **FIRST SPROUT** · **FIRST HARVEST** ("Stick your badge here on Tommy's harvest day!")

### 🌿 OKKI — THE SPEEDSTER (Okra)
- At a glance: night before planting — **soak seeds in plain water 8–12 hrs** · seed depth **12 mm** · sprouts **5–7 days** · grow bag water 400 ml (4 Cuppys) · best habit: **Harvest often!**
- Phase 1 Nursery (7 steps): ① Soak seeds overnight ② Soak Corky 5 mins ③ Fill nursery mix, 10 mm space ④ Poke hole **12 mm** deep (Diggy) ⑤ Drop 2 okra seeds, cover gently ⑥ Label "OKKI" + date ⑦ Mist 10–15 times. Warm sunny spot. "Okki sulks in cold."
- Milestones: every day mist, keep warm, never in cold · Days 5–7 sprouts · Weeks 2–3 stronger · Weeks 4–5 true leaves ready.
- Graduation: true leaves 2 sets · **thick** stem upright · white roots in cup · height 80–100 mm.
- Phase 2 Big Move: ① grow bag ② punch holes ③ **halfway fill with soil** ④ **place cup at surface level** ⑤ fill soil to 10 mm below rim ⑥ water 400 ml. *(⚠ p9 footnote implies Okki gets deep burial — see §7 conflicts.)*
- Phase 3 Grow & Harvest: Weeks 6–7 yellow pods appear · 7–10 pods 7–10 cm long, "Flowers keep coming!" · 8–12+ "Harvest regularly — more you pick, more it gives!" · PICK OFTEN: "Snip never twist. Wash hands after. Never touch eyes!"
- Badges: FIRST SPROUT · FIRST HARVEST.

### 🌶️ CHILLY — THE SLOW BURN (Chilli)
- At a glance: night before — nothing needed · seed depth **6 mm** · sprouts **10–14 days** ("normal — don't worry!") · grow bag water **300 ml (3 Cuppys)** *(⚠ conflicts with Phase-2 step 400 ml — §7)* · best habit: **Patience!**
- Theme: "Patient. Powerful. Worth the wait!" Flame aura. Slow sprouting is THE lesson.
- Phase 1 Nursery: ① Soak corky ② fill mix, 10 mm ③ poke 6 mm (Diggy) ④ drop 2 chilli seeds — "tiny, handle carefully!" ⑤ label "Chilly" + date ⑥ Mist 10–15 times. Warm sunny window. "Cold = no sprouting."
- Milestones: every day mist/keep warm/never cold · Days 10–14 sprouts ("keep calm") · Weeks 2–3 stronger · Weeks 3–4 true leaves ready.
- Graduation: true leaves 2 sets (long, narrow, pointed) · stem naturally slim — upright > thickness · white threads through cup walls · 80–100 mm (may need an extra week. Be patient!).
- Phase 2: same 6 steps as Okki — halfway fill, **place cup at surface level** — "Unlike Okki, Chilly stays at surface level - no deep burial needed."
- Phase 3: Weeks 8–10 small white flowers · 12–14 green chillies form · 14+ turn red and ripe · HARVEST OFTEN: "Snip never twist. Wash hands after. Never touch eyes!" · "Green or red, you choose!"
- Badges: FIRST SPROUT ("when Chilly sprouts!") · FIRST HARVEST.

### 🌱 METHI — THE FENUGREEK · THE QUICK GREENS
- Why special: **MANY PLANTS** · **NO TRANSPLANTING** · **FASTEST HARVEST** · **2 HARVESTS** ✌️
- Round 1 (Coir cup): ① Soak Corky ② fill cup with nursery mix ③ **SCATTER seeds** (pinch, no hole) ④ cover with **5 mm** soil ⑤ label METHI + date ⑥ mist.
- Round 1 timeline: Day 2–4 sprouts · Day 5–10 dense green · Week 2 taller · Week 3–4 ready · **HARVEST!** (cut with scissors, basket).
- Harvest test: "Smell your hands! That strong, slightly bitter smell? That's real fenugreek. You grew it!"
- Round 2 (Grow bag): ① punch 4–6 holes ② fill to **2 cm below rim** ③ scatter seeds ④ cover with 5 mm soil ⑤ label ⑥ **water 200 ml**.
- Round 2 timeline: Day 2–4 sprouts · Week 2 filling in · Week 3–4 cut · HARVEST AGAIN!
- **METHI'S GOLDEN RULE:** TWO ROUNDS · TWO HARVESTS · SAME SEED · BETTER GARDENER ⭐
- Badges: **ROUND 1 HARVEST!** · **ROUND 2 HARVEST!**
